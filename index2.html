About ZamListing

ZamListing Rides is a premium ride-hailing service operating in the Luangwa Feira region, providing fast, reliable, and affordable transportation solutions for all your travel needs.

Our Services

  • Motorbike Rides: Quick and efficient transportation for single passengers
  • Car Transportation: Comfortable and spacious vehicles for groups and families
  • Offline Map Navigation: Navigate without internet connection
  • Real-time Distance Calculation: Get accurate pricing before booking

How It Works

1. Enter your phone number
2. Select your preferred transport mode
3. Choose your pickup location
4. Select your destination
5. Review the trip summary and pricing
6. Submit your booking request

Service Areas

We operate in Luangwa with coverage of major locations including: Luangwa Bus Station, Luangwa Market, Luangwa Feira Rides, Katondwe Mission Hospital, and Airstrip areas.

Contact & Support

For inquiries and support, please contact us through the app or visit our office at Luangwa Feira Rides.
