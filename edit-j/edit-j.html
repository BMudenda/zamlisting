Psychosocial Counsellors, Mental Health Practitioners, and Nursing Assistants Needed Province-Wide

AVAILABLE POSITIONS:

  • Lusaka Province: 20 positions
  • Copperbelt Province: 15 positions
  • Southern Province: 15 positions
  • Central Province: 10 positions
  • Western Province: 20 positions
  • Muchinga Province: 10 positions
  • Luapula Province: 10 positions
  • Eastern Province: 10 positions
  • Northern Province: 10 positions

REQUIREMENTS:

  • CAZ Membership Certificate (mandatory for non-members)
  • CAZ Practicing Certificate
  • CAZ Recommendation Letter
  • Diploma, Certificate, or Degree in Counselling or related fields (e.g., Psychology, Social Work)
  • Relevant experience in counselling or nursing (at least 2 years)

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Provide counselling services to individuals, groups, and communities
  • Assess clients' needs and develop counselling plans
  • Monitor and record clients' progress
  • Liaise with other healthcare professionals
  • Participate in training and development programs
  • Provide nursing care and support to patients
  • Administer medications and treatments as prescribed
  • Maintain accurate records and reports
