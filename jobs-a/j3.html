POSITION: YOUTH CHILDREN’S MINISTRY COORDINATOR LOCATION: CHIPATA, EASTERN PROVINCE TYPE: FULL-TIME/CONTRACTUAL RENEWABLE BASED ON PERFORMANCE DEADLINE: 10TH APRIL 2025 ABOUT US:

Founded in 1995 after being curved from the Diocese of Lusaka, the Anglican Diocese of Eastern Zambia (ADEZ) is one of the five dioceses in Zambia. The Diocese has existed for nearly thirty (30) years, with two Bishops leading it at various stages. The Diocese elected a new Bishop in June 2024, marking a new phase of the Diocese’s Transformational Leadership and Sustainable Development.

The Diocese also runs Social Justice programs, Education, Health, and development. ADEZ believes in the power of missions and development through integrated Mission and Development to support strong families, Churches, and Communities (John 10:10b) to promote human dignity and holistic ministry.

To realise the dream and vision of ‘Transformational Leadership through Mission and Sustainable Development, the Diocese has established human resource and financial management systems to meet the aspirations of today’s organisational compliance mechanism to enhance efficiency and effective organisational development of ADEZ.

We are seeking a passionate, dynamic, detail-oriented, and results-oriented Diocesan Youth and Children Ministry Coordinator with multitasking experience to lead the growth phase program of the Anglican Diocese of Eastern Zambia Youth & Children’s Ministry.

ABOUT THE JOB
  • The Youth & Children’s Ministry Coordinator (YCMC) will work under the direction of the Diocesan Bishop and youth Chaplain and in collaboration with the Diocesan Youth Council, the Diocesan Standing Committee, the Archdeaconry and Parish Council and others to deliver on the objectives and actions of the Young People and Children Ministry plan in the Diocesan Pastoral Plan.
  • The role will involve creating, resourcing, coordinating, delivering, and supporting youth and children’s ministry programmes and initiatives across the Diocese. It will also include collaborating with schools and parishes to build local support.
  • To grow the Youth and Children’s Ministry of the Diocese of Eastern Zambia by creating and developing youth and children’s programmes. The post holder will significantly contribute to discipleship by developing relationships with young people within the church and the wider community.
  • They will further support and encourage other gifts and skills of young people and children through the various ministries of Boys’ Brigade, Girls’ Brigade, Girls’ Friendly Society, Sunday School, Praise Teams, Altar Servers, Church Choirs, and other groups that may be part of this vital ministry department, including those who do not to any Church Youth Groups but are under the age of 35 years (by Diocesan Youth definition).
